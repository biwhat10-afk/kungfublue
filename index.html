About

The
Practice

Bilal Hussain is a photographer drawn to the overlooked moment — moonlight through bare branches, a lone tree dissolving into fog, a child's red heart against a grey world.

These photographs are made slowly, with attention given to what most cameras walk past. Light on water. The weight of a quiet sky. The geometry of the unseen.
